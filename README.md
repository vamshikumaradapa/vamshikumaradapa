## 🚀 Entrepreneurial Journey

### 🌱 Founder — BioCycle Pads
> *Eco-Friendly Sanitary Pads Made from Agro Waste*

**BioCycle Pads** is on a mission to make menstrual hygiene more **affordable, accessible, and sustainable** by transforming agro waste into **biodegradable sanitary pads**. As the Founder, I lead the vision, product innovation, and strategic execution of the venture — combining sustainability with social impact.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- 🌾 Product research and development using agro waste
- 🌍 Driving sustainability and social impact initiatives
- 👥 Building and leading the founding team
- 📈 Strategic planning, partnerships, and growth

---

### 💨 Managing Director — OXYWALLS
> *Indoor Air Purifier Solutions for Healthier Living Spaces*

Serving as the **Managing Director** at **OXYWALLS**, a startup focused on developing **innovative indoor air purifier solutions** designed to improve air quality in homes, offices, and public spaces. I contribute to product strategy, operations, and driving the company's growth vision.

**Key Contributions:**
- 💡 Product strategy and innovation planning
- ⚙️ Overseeing operations and product development
- 🤝 Building partnerships and expanding reach
- 📊 Business development and growth strategy
